Revenue Leak Engine

A practical diagnostic layer for identifying where structural incompleteness suppresses flow, trust, completion, or recovery.

Engine role

The engine converts the Funnelplugs doctrine into a decision-support layer capable of exposing missing plugs and guiding intervention logic.

Governed Instrument

Revenue Leak Diagnostic Engine

A governed diagnostic instrument for identifying structural revenue leakage across digital funnels and determining the intervention logic required to restore flow, trust, clarity, and recovery.

Purpose

Structural Diagnosis

To classify structural leakage, score funnel integrity dimensions, and recommend the appropriate plug classes without resorting to decorative metrics or unsupported financial claims.

Method

Rule-Based / Auditable

This instrument is a structural diagnostic model. It does not produce financial forecasts, market benchmarks, or unsupported performance claims. It evaluates declared funnel conditions through governed scoring and decision logic.

Time

4 Minutes

20 governed questions across 5 diagnostic modules.

Method Boundaries

What This Instrument Does — And Does Not Do

It Does

  • Classify structural leakage

    Identify the dominant and secondary leak classes through governed scoring and explicit resolution rules.

  • Score integrity dimensions

    Measure trust, continuity, clarity, friction containment, and recovery readiness in a reviewable form.

  • Recommend intervention classes

    Map diagnosed structural weakness to governed plug classes without collapsing the result into shallow product recommendations.

It Does Not

  • No financial forecasting

    This instrument does not estimate revenue loss in absolute monetary terms in v1.

  • No market benchmarking

    This instrument does not compare your path against industry percentiles, market averages, or external performance datasets.

  • No black-box inference

    The output is governed by explicit rules. It is not generated through undocumented AI reasoning.

Module 1

Message and Intent Alignment

Evaluates whether pre-click promise, landing-page narrative, user intent, and next-step logic remain structurally aligned.

Q01

How clearly does your landing page continue the promise made before the click?

Measures continuity between pre-click intent and on-page structural fulfillment.

Q02

Does the visitor immediately understand what is being offered and for whom it is intended?

Measures whether the path establishes immediate interpretive clarity.

Q03

Is the next step on the page obvious without requiring interpretation?

Measures whether decision movement is structurally signposted.

Q04

Is there a visible mismatch between user intent and the action path offered on the page?

Measures whether the path structurally serves the intent that brought the visitor to it.

Module 2

Trust Integrity

Evaluates whether the path sustains confidence, legitimacy, reassurance, and proof at decision-critical moments.

Q05

How credible does the page feel at the moment of decision?

Measures perceived legitimacy and trustworthiness at commitment boundary.

Q06

How visible are trust signals such as proof, reassurance, legitimacy, or risk reduction?

Measures the presence of trust infrastructure visible to the user.

Q07

Does the visitor receive enough reassurance before committing to the next step?

Measures whether the path reduces hesitation before commitment.

Q08

How likely is hesitation caused by a lack of confidence in the page or offer?

Measures whether the page introduces trust-related hesitation instead of removing it.

Module 3

Flow and Friction

Evaluates continuity of movement, interruption risk, and the extent of unnecessary effort within the path.

Q09

How smooth is the progression from initial interest to action?

Measures structural continuity from intent to conversion action.

Q10

How much unnecessary effort is required to continue through the path?

Measures burden and drag introduced by avoidable process friction.

Q11

Are there points where the user is forced to stop, think, or re-orient unexpectedly?

Measures whether continuity is interrupted by structural disorientation.

Q12

How likely is drop-off caused by process complexity rather than offer weakness?

Measures whether process architecture itself is suppressing completion.

Module 4

Decision Clarity

Evaluates whether the visitor can understand the offer, the value, the choice, and the next action without ambiguity.

Q13

How clear is the value proposition at the decision point?

Measures whether the offer remains interpretable at the moment of commitment.

Q14

How easy is it to understand why this offer should be chosen now?

Measures urgency and choice logic intelligibility.

Q15

How clearly are options, outcomes, or commitments explained?

Measures whether the commitment boundary is explained without ambiguity.

Q16

How likely is hesitation caused by ambiguity rather than resistance?

Measures whether confusion, rather than objection, is suppressing movement.

Module 5

Recovery and Continuation

Evaluates whether the system is capable of restoring momentum after hesitation, interruption, or abandonment.

Q17

If a visitor hesitates or leaves, is there a structured recovery path in place?

Measures whether the system is structurally prepared to re-engage lost intent.

Q18

How strong is your follow-up continuity after interruption?

Measures whether interruption leads to structural continuity or silent decay.

Q19

Do visitors receive re-entry prompts, reminders, or reassurance after abandonment?

Measures whether the system attempts structured reactivation after abandonment.

Q20

How prepared is the system to restore lost intent rather than simply lose it?

Measures whether recovery is a built system property rather than an absence zone.
